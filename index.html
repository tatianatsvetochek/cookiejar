Funfetti Cookie 🎉

  • 2-1/4 cups flour (293 grams)
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon corn starch
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 1/4 cups plus 2 tablespoons granulated sugar (288 grams)
  • 14 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted (197 grams)
  • 1 tablespoon light corn syrup (20 grams)
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2-3/4 cup rainbow sprinkles (about 3-4 ounces, not nonpareils)

How to cook Funfetti Cookies:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  • In a bowl, cream ½ cup butter and ¾ cup sugar until fluffy
  • Mix in 1 egg and 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Stir in 1 ½ cups flour, ½ tsp baking soda, and ¼ tsp salt
  • Fold in ½ cup rainbow sprinkles
  • Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes

Chocolate Cookie 🍪

  • 1 cup (226g) butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200g) brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups (340g) chocolate chips

How to cook Chocolate Cookies:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  • Cream ½ cup butter and ¾ cup brown sugar
  • Add 1 egg and 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Mix in 1 cup flour, ¼ cup cocoa powder, ½ tsp baking soda, and ¼ tsp salt
  • Fold in ½ cup chocolate chips
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes
