Cookie Jar

Funfetti Cookie 🎉

  • 250g of butter
  • 200g of brown sugar
  • 100g of granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 300g of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • Rainbow sprinkles

How to cook Funfetti Cookies:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  • In a bowl, cream ½ cup butter and ¾ cup sugar until fluffy
  • Mix in 1 egg and 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Stir in 1 ½ cups flour, ½ tsp baking soda, and ¼ tsp salt
  • Fold in ½ cup rainbow sprinkles
  • Scoop dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes

Chocolate Cookie 🍪

  • 250g of butter
  • 200g of brown sugar
  • 100g of granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 300g of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 350g of chocolate chips

How to cook Chocolate Cookies:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  • Cream ½ cup butter and ¾ cup brown sugar
  • Add 1 egg and 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Mix in 1 cup flour, ¼ cup cocoa powder, ½ tsp baking soda, and ¼ tsp salt
  • Fold in ½ cup chocolate chips
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes

Pistacio Cookie 🌿

  • 250g of butter
  • 200g of brown sugar
  • 100g of granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 300g of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 2 cups (340g) of chopped pistachios

How to cook Pistacio Cookies:

  • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  • Cream ½ cup butter and ¾ cup brown sugar
  • Add 1 egg and 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Mix in 1 cup flour, ½ tsp baking soda, and ¼ tsp salt
  • Fold in 2 cups of chopped pistachios
  • Bake for 10-12 minutes
